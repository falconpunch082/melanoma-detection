1) What is Skin Cancer ?

The excessive exposure to the ultraviolet (UV) radiation from the sun can damage the skin cells, leading to the development of skin cancer. There are three main types of skin cancer:
  • Basal cell carcinoma
  • Squamous cell carcinoma
  • Melanoma

2) Symptoms and Causes

Knowing your skin and monitoring it for changes is key to observe symtoms of skin cancer. The follwing symtoms can be observed:
  • any crusty, non-healing sores
  • small lumps that are red, pale or pearly in colour
  • new spots, freckles or any moles changing in colour, thickness or shape over a period of weeks to months
